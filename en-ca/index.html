Competitive

Dedicated to coaching and training, fostering a love for swimming, and building community.

Our competitive program at CAMO NATATION is designed to provide our athletes with the training and education necessary to reach their full potential and achieve excellence in the sport. We believe that a well-rounded approach is key to success, and our program combines technical training, physical conditioning, and mental preparation to build champions.

Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned competitor, our competitive program is dedicated to helping you achieve your goals and reach your full potential.

High Performance

The High Performance group is a level of training for competitive swimmers who have shown a high level of dedication and skill in the sport of swimming.

Junior

The Juniors group is a level of training for competitive swimmers aged between 13 and 16 who have shown a passion for the sport and a willingness to be national champions.

Jeunesse & Sport √âtudes

The Youth group is a level of training for competitive swimmers aged between 10 and 12 who show potential in the sport of swimming and are willing to work hard to improve their skills. The training program for this group is designed to provide a foundation of technical and physical abilities while also developing the mental aspects of competitive swimming.

Cadet

This program for swimmers aged between 8 and 10 is designed to balance the work on the four strokes, endurance and strength training and mental preparation for competitions.
